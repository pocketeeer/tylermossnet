Spot, The Word Watcher

Spot, The Word Watcher is a Discord bot that watches user text conversations and analyses them to recognize interesting user speech patterns.
Everytime a user sends a message in a server, Spot will take it's content and look at specific characteristics of the text such as it's author, common user keywords, punctuation use, capitalization, current time, and more. It will save these characteristics and save it in the author's speech profile. The message is then discarded (no messages are recorded or saved), and Spot is ready for the next message.

To view a user's speech profile, simply use the command s, profile @user.
Speech profiles show all of the interesting statistics that Spot has gathered on the given user!

Details
What does Spot find?
  • author
  • favourite topics
  • vibe % (how positive/negative is this user?)
  • most commonly used words
  • properness % (how often does this user use proper punctuation and capitalization?)
  • time most active (both time of the day and day of the week are shown!)
